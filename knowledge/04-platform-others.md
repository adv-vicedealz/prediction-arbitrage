# Other Prediction Market Platforms

## PredictIt

### Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Founded** | 2014 |
| **Operator** | Victoria University of Wellington (New Zealand) |
| **Support** | Aristotle, Inc. |
| **Type** | Academic research prediction market |
| **Regulatory Status** | CFTC no-action letter (historically) |

### History & Regulatory Journey

**Original Structure (2014-2022)**:
- Operated under CFTC "no-action letter"
- Designed as nonprofit educational/research tool
- Required limitations:
  - 5,000 traders maximum per question
  - $850 cap per trader per question
  - Academic research purpose

**2022 Crisis**:
- August 2022: CFTC withdrew no-action letter
- Ordered PredictIt to cease operations within 6 months
- September 2022: Users and academics sued in Texas federal court
- Argued CFTC action was "arbitrary and capricious"

**Fifth Circuit Victory (2023)**:
- July 2023: Appeals court reversed district court
- Instructed entry of preliminary injunction against CFTC
- PredictIt allowed to continue operating

**2025 Resolution**:
- September 2025: PredictIt received full DCM and DCO approval
- Expected to launch expanded platform late 2025
- December 2025: Received no-action letter relief from CFTC data requirements

### Market Structure

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Contract Type** | Binary YES/NO |
| **Price Range** | $0.01 - $0.99 |
| **Fees** | 10% of profits + 5% withdrawal fee |
| **Position Limit** | $850 per contract |
| **Trader Limit** | 5,000 per market |

### Unique Value
- Academic credibility
- Research data for political science
- Conservative position limits reduce manipulation risk
- Long track record (10+ years)

## Metaculus

### Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Founded** | 2015 |
| **Founders** | Max Wainwright, Greg Laughlin, Anthony Aguirre |
| **Type** | Reputation-based forecasting platform |
| **Funding** | $5.5M (Open Philanthropy, 2022) + $300K (EA Fund, 2021) |
| **Structure** | Public benefit corporation |
| **Currency** | Reputation points (no real money) |

### How It Works

Unlike prediction markets with real money, Metaculus uses:

1. **Reputation Points**: Users earn/lose points based on prediction accuracy
2. **Community Prediction**: Aggregated forecast from all participants
3. **Metaculus Prediction**: Weighted aggregate favoring high-reputation forecasters
4. **Scoring**: Brier and Log scoring rules

### Accuracy Track Record

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Mean Brier Score (all questions) | 0.151 |
| Mean Brier Score (AI questions) | 0.207-0.230 |
| Forecaster Behavior | Some overconfidence noted |

**Key Finding**: Metaculus Prediction consistently outperforms median community prediction.

### Wisdom of Crowds Effect
- Community Prediction often beats individual forecasters
- Individual biases balance out in aggregate
- More accurate with larger participant pools

### Focus Areas

| Domain | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **AI/Technology** | Timing of AI milestones, tech breakthroughs |
| **Science** | Scientific discoveries, research outcomes |
| **Geopolitics** | International events, policy changes |
| **Pandemic** | Disease spread, public health |
| **Climate** | Environmental predictions |
| **Existential Risk** | Long-term civilization risks |

### Unique Value
- **No financial incentive**: Pure forecasting skill measurement
- **Long-term questions**: Can forecast years/decades ahead
- **Academic rigor**: Detailed track record analysis
- **Effective Altruism alignment**: Focus on important questions

### Limitations
- No real money stakes (may reduce commitment)
- Smaller user base than commercial platforms
- Questions can be niche/esoteric
- Not a trading platform

## Augur

### Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Founded** | 2014 (launched 2018) |
| **Type** | Decentralized prediction market protocol |
| **Blockchain** | Ethereum |
| **Token** | REP (Reputation) |
| **Status** | Pioneer, but declining relevance |

### Historical Significance
- First major decentralized prediction market
- Introduced concept of decentralized oracle resolution
- REP token for dispute resolution voting

### Challenges
- High gas fees on Ethereum mainnet
- Complex user experience
- Polymarket captured market share with Polygon L2
